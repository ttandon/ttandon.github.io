Groundbreaking Artificial Intelligence to analyze your health. Powered by your smartphone.




Medical diagnostics are ripe for disruption.

For the past 150 years, blood diagnostics have hardly changed. Blood tests are used to diagnose and identify over 20,000 of mankind’s deadliest diseases, and yet the process still remains costly and highly cumbersome. Athelas aims to change that - we’re working on a computer vision based solution that allows for cell analysis and identification on your smartphone.
